## Electrical Design *(Wiring Diagram, Electrical Schematic, and Component List)*
In this section, you can find the electrical diagrams and the list of components used for this project.
The system consists of an ESP32 microcontroller and a 24V DC power supply. It includes three NEMA 17 stepper motors with 59 Ncm torque, accompanied by three TB6600 drivers. The mechanical hardware comprises 25x25 mm aluminum profiles, 3D printed components, six 300 mm MGN12 linear guides, as well as 6 mm GT2 belts and belt tensioners.
